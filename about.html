ABOUT US

Welcome to Dashlane Corporation Pvt. Ltd., a global player in international trade since July 2023. Founded by our CEO, Mr. Abhishek Goswami.

We've rapidly expanded from our roots in the construction industry to become a dynamic force in the global market. Our diverse business interests span ship chartering, energies, agro-processed commodities, healthcare products, industrial machinery, and logistics.

At Dashlane Corporation, we are synonymous with excellence, innovation, and client satisfaction. We set new standards in international trade, serving clients worldwide with a commitment to quality and reliability. Join us as we navigate the seas of global commerce, connecting continents and facilitating the exchange of premium products across industries.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a dynamic global leader, pioneering innovation and excellence across a wide range of industries. We are dedicated to leveraging cutting-edge technologies, sustainable practices, and strategic partnerships to deliver exceptional value and drive progress. Our vision is to continuously explore new business ventures, expand our impact, and shape the future of diverse markets, creating a lasting legacy of growth and success.

Mission

Our mission is to be a driving force in global infrastructure development and international trade while continually expanding into new industries. We are committed to delivering excellence, innovation, and sustainability across all our projects and ventures. By fostering strong partnerships and embracing new opportunities, we aim to create lasting value, not only in the sectors we lead but also in emerging industries, contributing to a brighter, more connected future.
