Healthcare

Dashlane Corporation Pvt. Ltd. stands as a cornerstone in the healthcare products sector, specializing in the supply of vital medical devices, equipment, and pharmaceutical products. Our comprehensive portfolio features essentials such as cotton gauze, catheters, as well as a range of pharmaceutical innovations including antibiotics, pain relievers, and life-saving medications. With an unwavering commitment to excellence, Dashlane Corp ensures an unbroken supply chain, meeting critical healthcare needs globally.

  • Medical devices: Our advanced medical devices span from diagnostic equipment to patient care solutions, including test kits, thermometers, SpO2 measurements, glucose monitoring system portable X-ray machines, etc.
  • Pharmaceutical Products: Offering a diverse range, including antibiotics, pain relievers, generic medicines, life-saving medications, and specialized formulations for various medical needs.
  • Medical Equipments: We take care of all your first aid needs, providing you with as basic as bandages to cotton gauze and antiseptics as well. Dashlane Corporation aims to provide complete solution for your medical needs
  • Surgical Equipments: Our portfolio includes a variety of surgical instruments, such as scalpels, forceps, retractors, Scissors, needles, syringe, catheters, IV-fluid tubes, feeding tubes, etc. ensuring the highest standards in surgical precision.
